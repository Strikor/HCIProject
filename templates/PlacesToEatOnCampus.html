Places to Eat On Campus

Student Top Picks:

Metropolitan Deli

Grab a delicious sub, sandwich or salad from one of the two Metro Deli locations on campus.

Grill '72

Offers burgers made fresh to order and other grill favorites and seasoned tots.

More Options:

Eastway Center

Home to a large selection of grocery items. Enjoy a sandwich or wrap creation from the deli or grab a fresh brewed coffee or latte from the full-service beverage bar.

Fork In The Road Food Truck

Kent State's very own food truck!

DI Dining

Eat in or grab-n-go.

Rosie's Diner

New menu includes new deli and pizza options, or grab your favorite diner foods and decadent desserts.

Flash Bistro

Features local Bent Tree Coffee, pastries from the Kent State Bakeshop, fresh soup and grab-n-go items and more.

Hippie Chick'n

Offering homestyle breaded chicken tenders, a variety of sauces and scrumptious sides.

Homeslice Pizzeria

Pizza can be served by the slice or the whole pie.

Einstein Bros. Bagels

The neighborhood bagel shop is on campus, serving freshly bakes bagels, sandwiches, coffee, and more.

Wild Blue Sushi

Offers authentic Japanese rolls that are made daily by a certifies sushi chef.

Tahini

Mediterranean cuisine located at the Rathskeller and includes hummus wraps, falafel, lemonade, and baklava.

George T. Simon III Cafe

Delicious, healthy flatbreads, sandwiches, grab-n-go and breakfast options.

Summit Street Cafe

Salads, specialty sandwiches, beverages, and more loctaed in the Student Recreation & Wellness Center.

The Charging Station

Enjoy unique flatbreads, sandwichesm breakfast items, and more.

Starbucks

Coffeehouse chain known for its signature roasts, light bites, and WiFi availability.
