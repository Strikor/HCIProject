Kent, Ohio experiences a temperate climate with distinct seasons. Winters are cold, and often times have snowfall and low Temptures, while summers are warm and can be humid. Spring and fall showcase milder temperatures with bright colorful foliage. Due to Kent's inland location, Kent can occasionally witness tempertaure extremes, with occasional winter storms and hot summer days. Residents of Kent can expect to enjoy a variety of weather conditions throughout the year.
